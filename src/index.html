Shortcuts list

  • Esc - Close the shortcuts list
  • R - Record / Stop recording
  • Spacebar - Play/Pause
  • Left Arrow - Rewind 1 second
  • Right Arrow - Fast Forward 1 second
  • Up Arrow - Go to the end of the video
  • Down Arrow - Go to the beginning of the video
  • Shift + Left Arrow - Rewind 0.1 second
  • Shift + Right Arrow - Fast Forward 0.1 second
  • Shift + R - Go to the beginning of the video and start recording
